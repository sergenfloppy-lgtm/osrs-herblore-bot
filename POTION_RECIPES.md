# OSRS Herblore Potion Recipes

**Complete reference for herblore training**

---

## How Potion Making Works

1. Use **HERB** + **SECONDARY INGREDIENT** = **FINISHED POTION**
2. Each potion gives **XP** based on level requirement
3. Can make 14 potions per inventory (14 herbs + 14 secondaries)

## Training Path

**Recommended progression:**
1. **Level 3-15:** Attack potions (cheap, low XP)
2. **Level 15-26:** Serum 207 (if doing quest)
3. **Level 26-38:** Energy potions or Quest rewards
4. **Level 38-55:** Prayer potions (good profit/XP)
5. **Level 55-72:** Super strength (good XP)
6. **Level 72-81:** Ranging potions (high XP)
7. **Level 81-99:** Saradomin brews (highest XP)
